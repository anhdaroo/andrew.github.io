The Wim Hof Method is about reconnecting us - to ourselves, to others and to nature. This Method is based on three powerful pillars: Breathing, Cold Therapy, and Commitment.  

My experiences of Meditation...

I've been practicing the Wim Hof Method for 3 months now. Every morning I wake up and perform the breathing exercises and conclude the meditation with a 2 minute cold shower. After having done this for a few months, I can confidently say that I am more aware, awake, and energized after this session. It has helped changed my life and has given me prolonged energy throughout the day.

My experiences with Nature...

I took a trip to Utah to hike up Angel's Landing. This is a heavily trafficked out and back trail which is rated as difficult. I hiked 4.4 miles and scaled 1,604 ft to reach the top. This is one of my most memorable hikes because of how difficult it is for one, but the amount of comradery there are amongst the hikers. Everyone was super motivating and only helped pushed me to accomplish this feat.
